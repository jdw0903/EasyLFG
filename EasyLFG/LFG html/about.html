About EasyLFG
A small project with simple goals: fast, private, and stress-free group finding.

EasyLFG is a no-login, lightweight LFG tool for any game. It’s designed so you can open the site, post what you’re playing, and find people to play with in just a few clicks—no accounts, no tracking, no bloat.

Design principles

  • Always fast & lightweight.
    No heavy frameworks on the front-end, no giant bundles. The site is just HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript talking to a small Node/Express API.
  • No account required.
    You don’t need to log in to use EasyLFG. Posts are identified with a secret token stored in your browser, so you can still delete your own posts.
  • Privacy-first.
    The only “contact” information you share is what you choose to put in your post (like a Discord handle or Steam name). There are no trackers, no social logins, and no third-party analytics baked into the UI.
  • Minimal, dark-mode by default.
    The interface is intentionally clean: a single screen for creating posts and browsing groups, always in dark mode so it looks good next to any game.
  • Resilient & transparent.
    If the backend is offline or your connection drops, EasyLFG shows clear messages and keeps your last loaded results instead of silently failing.
  • Security-minded.
    Input is validated and sanitized, posts are stored server-side, and deletion uses per-post secret tokens so random users can’t remove someone else’s listing.

How it works

When you create a post, the server generates a unique ID and a secret token. The token is stored only in your browser, and it’s required to delete that post later. Posts automatically expire after a configurable time-to-live (TTL), keeping the list fresh and focused on people who are actually online.

Filters, sorting, “Now only” mode, and basic matching (by game, platform, region, and time window) all run on the client, so browsing feels instant even with lots of posts.
